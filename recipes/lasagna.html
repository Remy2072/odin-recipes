Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish known for its layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, savory filling, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
